Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - StudyCore

Welcome to the StudyCore FAQ page. Here you'll find answers to common questions about our website and services. If you can't find the answer you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact our support team.

General Questions

What is StudyCore?
StudyCore (studycore.kesug.com) is a website designed to provide educational resources, study materials, learning tools, etc. for free now.
Do I need to create an account to use StudyCore?
An account is optional. Currently, no account is required to access most of our content. However, in the future, certain features might require registration.
Is StudyCore free to use?
Yes, StudyCore is currently free to use. We may introduce optional premium features in the future.
Where is my data stored?
As of now, all data related to your use of StudyCore is stored locally on your own system. We do not store any personal data on our servers during your general use of the website. For information on the limited data we collect through support and feedback forms, please see our Privacy Policy.

Content Usage

Can I share the content I find on StudyCore?
The content on StudyCore is intended for your personal, non-commercial, and educational use only. Sharing the content on platforms like Telegram or other mediums, especially for monetary benefit or in a way that could cause loss to StudyCore, is strictly prohibited. All content is copyrighted. Please refer to our Terms of Service for more details.
Can I use the content for commercial purposes?
No, the content on StudyCore is not to be used for any commercial purposes without explicit written permission from us. All rights are reserved.
Can I modify the content?
Modifying the content is not permitted without prior written consent from StudyCore. The integrity of the original content is important for maintaining its educational value and protecting our copyright.

Support

I'm experiencing a technical issue. What should I do?
If you encounter a technical issue, please describe the problem in detail through our Support, and our team will look into it as soon as possible. Please provide as much information as possible, including your browser and operating system.
How can I contact support?
The best way to contact our support team is through the Support available on our website. This allows us to track and address your concerns efficiently.

Feedback

How can I provide feedback on the website?
We welcome your feedback! Please use our feedback form to share your suggestions, comments, or report any issues.
Do you consider user feedback?
Yes, we highly value user feedback and use it to improve StudyCore and make it more helpful for everyone.
